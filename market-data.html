Market Data

This section contains historical and live market data sourced from MetaTrader 4 (MT4) and XTB APIs. It serves as a foundation for quantitative research, predictive modeling, and real-time trading strategies.

Available Data

  • High-Frequency Trading Data (HFD)
  • Daily/Intraday Historical Data (Forex, Indices, Commodities)
  • Economic Indicators and Macroeconomic Data (planned)

Current Progress

✅ Automated fetching system via Python & MT4 bridge implemented.
✅ Raw market data archived and structured for MongoDB integration.
⏳ Preparing advanced visualization dashboards.

Future Developments

Upcoming improvements include:

  • Streaming live data feeds into real-time analytics apps.
  • Cleaning and enriching raw datasets for data mining applications.
  • Building publicly accessible dashboards for selected indicators.
