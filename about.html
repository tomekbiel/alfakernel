About the Project

This platform combines fintech, data science, and automated trading systems. It reflects a long-term vision to integrate Python, R, MT4, and XTB APIs for efficient market analysis, strategy backtesting, and real-time decision making.

Vision

Building a modular and scalable research and trading ecosystem, using high-frequency financial data, statistical models, machine learning, and cloud deployment in the future.

Technologies

Python, R, MongoDB, JSON, Anaconda, MetaTrader 4, XTB API, Power BI, Jupyter, Colab, Shiny, Plotly, and others will power the system.

Mission

Combining academic rigor with practical market applications to build a sustainable fintech research hub and future trading automation systems.
